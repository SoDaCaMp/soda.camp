School of what, exactly?

soda.camp is a fusion of data literacy and computational art. This is an initiative of socratic learning, supporting communal movements, citizen ethics and open knowledge. For examples of what we mean by 'Open Data Arts', scroll down to the Gallery to see works produced with intent to provoke or inspire, where the underlying data and code is openly available for republishing, remixing and other uses.

This initiative is closely aligned with the Swiss association Opendata.ch. For information on upcoming events, visit #makeopendata and School of Data Switzerland, follow @sodacamper - or get in touch.
