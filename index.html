School of Data arts

This is a collaboration to promote a fusion of data literacy and computational art. A new initiative of socratic learning, supporting communal movements, citizen ethics and knowledge openness. To see what we mean by 'Data Arts', visit the Gallery to see works produced with intent to provoke or inspire, where openness is furthered when the underlying data and code is freely available for republishing, remixing and other uses. This project is aligned with the Swiss association Opendata.ch and the international School of Data. For more information, please contact us.
